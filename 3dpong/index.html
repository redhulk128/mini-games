3D Pong

This version of pong reminds squash a bit, the two paddles are in a cubical room,
except for that, the rules are exactly the same as the classic pong game, you should hit the ball with your paddle and move it to the opponent's ground, you will lose a point once you missed a ball. Move the paddle with the mouse.
